The Day of Danbury Festival

June 27th – 28th

1 Ives Street
Danbury, CT

The Day of Danbury Festival is a free, two-day outdoor event held on the Danbury Green on June 27th and 28th. It brings together live music, cultural performances, local vendors, and community programming open to everyone. The festival is designed to reflect the full range of people and cultures that call Danbury home.
