About

Day of Danbury is a celebration of the people and cultures that make Danbury, CT one of the most diverse cities in New England. It honors the immigrants, families, and communities who have built their lives here and shaped the city into what it is today.

Festival

The Day of Danbury Festival is a free, two-day outdoor event held on the Danbury Green on June 27th and 28th. It brings together live music, cultural performances, local vendors, and community programming open to everyone. The festival is designed to reflect the full range of people and cultures that call Danbury home.

The Danbury Benefit

The Danbury Benefit is a ticketed charity event featuring live performances and the exclusive premiere of a documentary celebrating Danbury's cultural story. All net proceeds are distributed among three local nonprofits working to strengthen the community.

The New American Dream Foundation

Empowering immigrant and first-generation communities through advocacy, resources, and opportunity.

The funds raised will directly support the Senior Hot Meal Program, which delivers monthly meals and friendly visits to more than 650 seniors living in Danbury's tax-credit housing communities. Each meal helps ensure that older adults not only receive nourishment, but also the companionship and human connection that many seniors living alone deeply need.

The Center for Empowerment & Ed.

Breaking the cycle of interpersonal violence through empowerment, education, and support.

Headquartered in Danbury, CEE is the only state-certified provider of DV and sexual assault services across 11 towns in Northern Fairfield and Southern Litchfield Counties. They serve over 30,000 individuals annually with 24/7 crisis intervention, shelter, and counseling — all at no cost.

Living Traditions Foundation Inc.

Preserving cultural heritage through arts, storytelling, and community programming.

LTF empowers first-time entrepreneurs in hospitality and the arts while celebrating Danbury's cultural richness. As the event host and organizer, LTF produces all aspects of Day of Danbury including programming, vendor selection, creative direction, and financial administration.
